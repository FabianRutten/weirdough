A little story

One day while sitting and marathon watching movies, nothing sounded better than a delicious baked good. Rummaging through the kitchen the only thing I could find was for a savory dinner menu and thought, why not? Thus Weirdough emerged; mouth watering homemade baked goods with a little bit of weird. Each treat is made with irregular ingredients, but that is not to say they are not scrumptious. The weird ingredients enhance the flavor of the treats giving them great power, and in return giving me great responsibility to share them with the world. All the treats are baked in the comfort of my own home, so you know they are made with dancing in the kitchen, singing while making them joy. Once they are perfectly prepared, I deliver them right to you. A weird delicious dessert with all the love of a home kitchen delivered right to your door to enjoy. How can it get any better?
